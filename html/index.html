About

Aaron Waldman hails from York, Maine, where 60's & 70's folk still reigns supreme.

The pathos of artists ranging from Jim Morrison to Manchester Orchestra to Kendrick Lamar inspired Aaron to craft a painfully earnest indie-folk sound reminiscent of James Taylor and Elliott Smith.

Aaron is currently building a following in New York City: Like the facebook page for updates on shows near you!
